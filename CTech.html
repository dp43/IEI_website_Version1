    MESSAGE FROM MESSAGE FROM FACULTY ADVISOR
    Dr. M. UMA DEVI
    FIE, C.TECH
    The Computer Engineering Divisions of the IE(I) Kattankulathur Local Centre SRMIST oversee the operations of this IE(I) Students' Chapter. The Chapter wants to educate aspiring engineers and fellow engineers. In May 2022, we opened the Chapter with 70 students. I extend an invitation to all student members to take part in technical activities hosted by IE(I) and ask that they do so with financial assistance from the Kolkata headquarters of IE(I). We invite renowned lecturers and business experts to participate in such events and impart their knowledge.

    We allow students the freedom to plan a variety of events, both during and outside of class time. Students develop leadership traits, soft skills, and teamwork. Having duties helps people develop their management and leadership abilities to deal with huge groups of people. With opportunities like being the MC, they may interact with the visitors and hone their communication skills while also developing their public speaking abilities.

    Students can take advantage of a discount on the registration price and take part in several activities. Every year on September 15th, Engineers Day, the IE(I) Kattankulathur Local Centre SRMIST hosts an award celebration to inspire the students. The students are judged based on how many IE(I) events they organized, how many events they participated in, and how many activities they organized/participated in outside of SRM. Additionally, the 10 students from our chapter are nominated yearly for Kattankulathur Local Center's Best IE(I) Student Chapter.

    I'm pleased to report that our chapter now has more than 860 pupils. To double the size of our Chapter, I ask all student members to induct more new members under the motto "Every Member Induct a New Member" into the IE(I) Students' Chapter. As an IE(I) Faculty Advisor, I must acknowledge the contributions of the faculty co-ordinators, current and previous Office-bearers along with corporate members and student heads of various activities for their valuable support and contributions. I am very grateful to our Management, The Dean (CET), our Chairperson (SCO), our Head of the Department (CTECH) and Chairman & Secretary of KTRLC for supporting us in all aspects.
    MESSAGE FROM FACULTY COORDINATORS
    Dr. J.JAYAPRADHA
    MIE
    A rich blend of technical leadership and social consciousness is entrusted to budding engineers by the IE(I) Student Chapter in collaboration with Computing Technologies in order to aid in the development of the skillful engineers. The accomplishments of the IE(I) student chapter are impressive and greatly valued. Our local chapter is very active and provides IEI members with lots of opportunities and provide the route to a desired career destination. All IE(I) team members and organizing students have gained knowledge of the professional procedures required for holding events, from making brochures to writing event summaries. We are grateful to IEI, the Kolkata headquarters, our chairman, secretary, head of department (CTECH), faculty advisor, and the rest of our team.
    The IE(I) Students’ Chapter, Department of Computing Technologies is established on 19th May 2022 with just 70 members. With extensive membership drive, this membership has increased 860+ members now. CTECH IEI Students’ chapter is highly active by conducting at least two activity almost every month in various categories resulting into about 23 events organized so far for the past one year time period. As a IE(I) Faculty coordinator, I must acknowledge the contributions of current and previous Office-bearers along with corporate members and student heads of various activities for their valuable contributions. I would like to thank our Management, The Dean(CET), our Chairperson(SCO) and our Head of the Department(CTECH) for providing us the opportunities to explore our students’ abilities.
