    The Institute of Engineers (India)

    IE(I) Kattankulathur Local Center

    The Institution of Engineers (India) is a non-profit organization supporting various technical activities, with its headquarters at Kolkatta. The Institution of Engineers (India), Kattankulathur is the youngest 16th local center, inaugurated in the centenary year of Tamilnadu State Center in December 2019. This local center actively organizes webinars, workshops, and other technical activities since its inception.

    The latest issue of Volume 3 (Issue 2) of IE(I) Kattankulathur Local Centre showcases the various events held by different divisions within the SRM Institute of Science and Technology from January to March 2023. As a part of the committee of IE(I) Kattankulathur Local Chapter, we are honored to organize numerous technical events and engage in various student activities through well-established associations with a long-standing reputation.

    Our local chapter is highly active and offers opportunities for IEI members. All committee members enthusiastically propose and organize technical-funded events. They have learned professional practices necessary for conducting events, ranging from creating brochures to preparing event reports. We would like to express our gratitude to IEI, Kolkatta headquarters for their encouragement in organizing more funded events.

    The technical events such as All India Workshops, Seminars, Quizzes, and Hackathons provide students with the chance to acquire new knowledge and explore subjects beyond their curriculum. The primary beneficiaries are students from diverse engineering disciplines. Consequently, our current activities at the local center foster a robust multidisciplinary culture among all the stakeholders involved.

    Membership Benefits

    1. Opportunity to participate in technical events organized by IEI State/Local Centres.
    2. Participation in various technical activities of the students' chapter.
    3. Access to library facilities at 121 State/Local Centres of the Institution across the country.
    4. Opportunity to meet fellow engineers for professional interaction.
    5. Reimbursement of travelling expenses to students selected for presentation of technical papers in Technicians/Students' Convention.
    6. After passing the final year examination, student members are eligible to become Associate Members of the Institution.
    7. Student Members of engineering colleges/polytechnics are eligible to participate in Students Design Competition every year being organized by National Design and Research Forum.
    8. Associate Members are entitled to receive Chartered Engineer certificate on payment of requisite fee.
    9. 85 scholarships of Rs 7,15,000/- given to the engineering meritorious students attached to the different Student chapters.
    10. Dr Amitava Bhattacharyya Memorial Trophy awarded to three Best Engineering College Students' Chapters and three Best Polytechnics Students' Chapters annually.
    11. The National Design and Research Forum (NDRF) conducts the students Design Competition every year amongst the student members studying in engineering colleges of the country.
    12. Best three technical papers presented in the Seminar and Technical Sessions by student members of Engineering College and Polytechnic Students' Chapters are awarded in the Annual Convocation and Technicians' Students' Convention.
    13. Participation in All India Students' Committee election.
    14. B P Kapadia Memorial Prizes and upto Rs 27,000/- to be awarded to the best three participants in Technical Session and Seminar during Student chapters' convention of Engineering College, Polytechnic Colleges and Technician Chapter separately.
    15. Engg Talks launched for student chapter members as community to interact amongst the engineering fraternity.

    Oppurtunities

    Our IEI organizers contribute significantly to the computing technology department through their efforts in organizing conferences, standardizing technologies, publishing research papers, promoting education, and advocating ethical practices. Their contributions have played a pivotal role in shaping the landscape of computing technology and driving its continuous evolution. They are also responsible for hosting numerous technical conferences and symposiums focused on computing technology. These events provide a platform for researchers, professionals, and academics to share their latest findings, discuss emerging trends, and collaborate on cutting-edge research. The exchange of ideas in these conferences has fostered innovation and pushed the boundaries of computing technology. They are actively involved in educational initiatives that promote the development of skills and knowledge in computing technology. This includes organizing workshops, webinars, and training sessions aimed at both students and professionals.
    By fostering education and training, IEI contributes to the continuous improvement of the workforce in the computing industry. Our Organizers work towards promoting responsibility and ethical practices within the computing technologies department .This includes addressing issues related to privacy, security, and the responsible use of emerging technologies. We the organisers have a strong sense of unity, responsibility and team building skills and contribute to our organization and the society in all aspects that we can.
